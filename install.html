How to install on your phone
1
Open the link above in Chrome
2
Tap the three dots (⋮) menu in the top right
3
Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4
Tap Install — the GEM gem icon will appear on your home screen
5
Open the app and sign in with the credentials you received
1
Open the link above in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
2
Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen (the box with an arrow pointing up)
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — the GEM gem icon will appear on your home screen
5
Open the app and sign in with the credentials you received
⚠️ On iPhone, you must use Safari — installing from Chrome or other browsers is not supported by Apple.
Your sign-in details will be shared with you separately.
Contact your GEM admin if you need help.
